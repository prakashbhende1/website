Resume of the Artist: Mr.Prakash Bhende.

Mr. Prakash Bhende born on 10th May 1942 has been a painter for more than 40 years.

He specializes in fresco style paintings. Frescoes are inscribed cave paintings prevalent since 5th century.

The caves of Ajanta & Ellora are fine examples of Fresco painting. Surprisingly ,45 years ago when Prakash Bhende had just heard of Ajanta/Ellora and cave temples in India, he discovered that his paintings had close resemblance with those present in this caves.Being a versatile painter he decided to specialize on Fresco Style art technique and began his own study of cave temples of India.

1962 He was Chief Designer for major Textile Companies viz. Garden Silk Mills, Reliance Group, Standard Mills, Vijaylin & Ellora Silk Mills.

1967 He Started career as a Painter.

1968 He held his First Solo Exhibition of Paintings at Chetna Jahangir Art Gallery which was inaugurated by Shri Acharya Atre.( A Great Indian leader)

1978 Introduced the Multi-colour screen printing on Sunmica for the first time in India for Caprihans Ltd. and Standard Laminates.

1979 Started a Film Production and Distribution Company. Since then produced and directed Six well-known Marathi Feature Films. A Well-known and respected personality in Marathi Film Industry. Introduced many Artist and Technicians viz. Nana Patekar, Sudesh Bhosale, Resham Sheth & etc.
