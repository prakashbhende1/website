Mr. Prakash Bhende born on 10th May 1942 has been a painter for more than 45 years.He specializes in fresco style paintings. Frescoes are inscribed cave paintings prevalent since 5th century.

The caves of Ajanta & Ellora are fine examples of Fresco painting. Surprisingly, 45 years ago when Prakash Bhende had just heard of Ajanta/Ellora and cave temples in India, he discovered that his paintings had close resemblance with those present in this caves.Being a versatile painter he decided to specialize on Fresco Style art technique and began his own study of cave temples of India. his expertise in fine art figarative and as well as abstract painting in various style.
